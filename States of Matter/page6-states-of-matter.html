The Four Classical States

Matter primarily exists in four classical states: solid, liquid, gas, and plasma. Each state has distinct physical properties.

Watch the Video

Key Characteristics

  • Solids: Fixed shape and volume
  • Liquids: Definite volume, shape of container
  • Gases: No fixed shape or volume
  • Plasma: Ionized gas, conducts electricity
